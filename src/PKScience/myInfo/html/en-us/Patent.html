Gear transmission
    Function:
  • 1.Power transmission
  • 2.Change direction of power source
  • 3.Change speed of gear rotation
    How does it work？
  • A gear (or cogwheel) is a rotating machine part having cut teeth, or cogs, which mesh with another toothed part to transmit torque. When two gears mesh, if one gear is bigger than the other, a mechanical advantage is produced, with the rotational speeds and the torques of the two gears differing in proportion to their diameters.

Solar cell
  • A solar cell is an electrical device that converts the energy of light directly into electricity by the photovoltaic effect, which is a physical and chemical phenomenon. Solar cell goes with gearing empower car to run. Solar cells generally work well with natural sunlight. Since artificial sources of light such as incandescent and fluorescent bulbs mimic the Sun’s spectrum, solar cells can also work indoors. Other artificial sources such as LED and neon lamps have very restricted color spectra; solar cells may not work as effectively with their light.
Hydraulic power
  • Hydraulic power is the use of fluids under pressure to generate, control, and transmit power. A hydraulic arm works by using high fluid pressure, created by a pump, to force a piston in a cylinder to move. As a valve is opened one way, the fluid is allowed to enter the cylinder and force the piston to move.
